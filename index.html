🐰 Follow the Bunny!

For parents:

Sit your child in front of the screen so their face is clearly visible in the camera. Then press start and say:

"Watch the bunny — wherever it goes, follow it with your eyes!"

The bunny will hop around for about 20 seconds. If your child looks away, the bunny will wait and make a sound until they look back. You don't need to do anything else.
