🌟 Meet the Magical Creatures!

For parents & clinicians:

Five magical creatures — Starby, Bubbles, Fizzwick, Roary and Shimmer — will appear one at a time around the screen. Each creature plays a little jingle when it arrives and bounces until it's been fed.

Say to the child:
"Can you find the magical creature? Look at it — feed it!"

The child just needs to look at each creature as it appears. The gold ring around the creature fills automatically — no button needed. A celebration burst plays when each creature is fed! 🎉

If it's not working, try moving closer to the screen, improving the lighting, or pressing Try Again. After two attempts a Skip option appears.
